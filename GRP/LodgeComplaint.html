Frequently Asked Questions

  • Before filing a complaint, make sure you're eligible to file a complaint and that you fulfill the following criteria:

    1. You should be resident of Patiala or reside in a block or village under the jurisdiction of Patiala district. The full list can be checked here.

    2. You're above 18 years of age

    3. That your complaint concerns the said department. If a complaint is filed againt the wrong department, it may be rejected outright

  • Your User Id is a unique number alloted at the time of registration. You can check it at the LogIn screen

  • Do not leave any of the fields blank

  • You may recieve an email, message or phone call on your registered mobile number, in regards to your complaint
